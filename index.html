About P.H.R.O.N.O.S.

Our Mission

P.H.R.O.N.O.S. is a research initiative dedicated to understanding the decline of strategic foresight capacity in high-noise information environments.

Core Values

  • Epistemic Humility — Acknowledging limits of knowledge
  • Radical Transparency — All methods and data public
  • Open Science — Freely accessible research

Living Documents

Our research evolves with evidence. We maintain transparent version control and invite critique and collaboration from the community.

Key Research Areas

01

Strategic Foresight

Measuring institutional capacity for long-term planning and horizon scanning in an age of information overload.

02

Signal vs. Noise

Understanding how to filter meaningful information from high-noise environments and make evidence-based decisions.

03

Institutional Resilience

Building frameworks for organizations to adapt, learn, and maintain foresight capacity in uncertain futures.

04

Epistemic Health

Developing methodologies for assessing and improving how organizations understand and validate knowledge claims.
